About OUTLAWED

Since its inception in 2006, Outlawed has steadily grown into one of the largest and most anticipated cultural fests not just among the Indian law schools but also among other undergraduate colleges in Eastern India. A wide assortment of events like the Great Kolkata Race, The Fashion Show, the Turncoat Debate, Chords of Fusion: Eastern and Western vocals and many more have drawn phenomenal participation year after year. This time, Outlawed 2016 promises to up the ante with the addition of brilliant new events. In the wintry evenings of the fest, students from across the country gather in festivity for concerts by the who’s who of the Indian music scene. Over the last couple of years, NUJS has had the pleasure of hosting bands like Indian Ocean, MIDIval Punditz Advaita, Junkyard Groove, Fire on Dawson, Raghu Dixit Project, Indus Creed, Dualist Inquiry, Agnee and Parikrama.

Battle of the Bands

Win this epic Band Battle by performing for your fans, playing live without the jive and showcasing the art of your own musicality.

THE GREAT KOLKATA RACE

Explore the City of Joy with your friends; Solve riddles, perform tasks and perform them fast. It’s the Amazing Race after all.

Vini Quizzi Vici

Annual NUJS Open Quiz Tournament. Think you know it all? Prove it – Come, Quiz, and Conquer.

Dance Project

The Group Dance. Form your troupe, find your rhythm and show us what you got! Battle it out with the best dance crews in India.

Spotlight

The Stage Play. Let the creative juices flow and cook up a story that sets our hearts ablaze. The world is a stage.

Fashionista

The Fashion Show. Strut your staff and walk away with insane prizes. Make your presence known.
